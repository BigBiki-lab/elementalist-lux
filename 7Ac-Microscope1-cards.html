Microscope 1

Microscope 1

Piece of equipment used to magnify small things.

Image

What you see when looking down a microscope.

Magnify

To make something look bigger.

Focus

To make an image clear and sharp. If an image is ‘in focus’ it is clear and sharp.

Specimen

The object you look at using a microscope.

Eyepiece lens

Part of the microscope you look down.

Slide

Glass sheet that a specimen is put on.

Stain

Dye used to colour parts of a cell to make them easier to see.
